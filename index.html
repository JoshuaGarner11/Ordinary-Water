THE GOOD NEWS

Four symbols. One message.

Tap each one to open it. It changes everything.

You're not an accident. Your life has purpose and meaning because God made you — and He wants a real relationship with you.

01

He made you

“For you created my inmost being; you knit me together in my mother's womb. I praise you because I am fearfully and wonderfully made.”
— Psalm 139:13-14

You were designed on purpose, for a purpose — unique, valuable, and deeply loved.

02

He loves you

“For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life.”
— John 3:16

God's love isn't based on what you've done. It's based on who He is.

03

He wants to know you

“Here I am! I stand at the door and knock. If anyone hears my voice and opens the door, I will come in.”
— Revelation 3:20

God isn't distant or angry. He's knocking on the door of your heart right now.

Here's the problem. We've all fallen short of God's perfect standard — that's what the Bible calls sin. And sin creates a gap between us and God.

01

Sin touches everything

“For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God.”
— Romans 3:23

Sin isn't just the “big” stuff. It's anything that falls short of God's perfect standard.

02

Sin separates us

“But your iniquities have separated you from your God; your sins have hidden his face from you.”
— Isaiah 59:2

This is the core problem — a wall between us and God that we can't tear down on our own.

03

Sin costs us life

“For the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord.”
— Romans 6:23

The “death” here isn't only physical — it's being separated from God. But notice the second half: there's a gift.

God loved you so much that He sent Jesus to fix what sin broke — to tear down the wall Himself.

01

Jesus came to give you life

“I have come that they may have life, and have it to the full.”
— John 10:10

Jesus didn't just come to rescue you from death — He came to give you a full, restored life.

02

Jesus died for your sins

“But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us.”
— Romans 5:8

Jesus took the punishment you deserved so you could be forgiven and restored.

03

Jesus rose from the dead

“He is not here; he has risen, just as he said.”
— Matthew 28:6

The resurrection proves Jesus is who He said He was — and that death has been defeated.

Jesus paid the price. Now the question is yours — what will you do with that?

01

Come to Jesus

“Come to me, all you who are weary and burdened, and I will give you rest.”
— Matthew 11:28

Jesus isn't waiting for you to get your life together first. He meets you right where you are.

02

Confess & believe

“If you declare with your mouth, ‘Jesus is Lord,’ and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved.”
— Romans 10:9

Salvation isn't about being perfect. It's about trusting Jesus with your life.

03

Call on His name

“Everyone who calls on the name of the Lord will be saved.”
— Romans 10:13

You don't need a special formula. Just talk to God honestly, from your heart.
